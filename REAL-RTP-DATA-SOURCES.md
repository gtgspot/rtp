- **International**: RTP may vary by jurisdiction

### RTP vs. Reality

‚ö†Ô∏è **Critical Understanding**:

1. **RTP is theoretical** - Calculated over billions of spins
2. **Short-term variance is normal** - Individual sessions will deviate significantly
3. **Volatility matters** - High RTP doesn't mean frequent wins
4. **House edge persists** - Even 99% RTP means 1% house edge over infinite play
